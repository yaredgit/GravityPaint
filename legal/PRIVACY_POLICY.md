# Privacy Policy for Gravity Paint

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Gravity Paint ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Game Progress**: Level completion, scores, and achievements are stored locally on your device.
- **Settings Preferences**: Sound, music, and difficulty preferences are stored locally.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect gameplay statistics to improve game balance and user experience.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Save and restore your game progress
- Improve game performance and fix bugs
- Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage

All game data (progress, settings, scores) is stored **locally on your device**. We do not transmit personal gameplay data to external servers unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud save features (if available).

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:
- **Analytics**: To understand how players interact with the game
- **Crash Reporting**: To identify and fix technical issues

These services have their own privacy policies governing their data collection practices.

## Children's Privacy

Gravity Paint is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal data input.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all game data by uninstalling the application
- Reset game progress through the in-game settings menu
- Disable analytics through your device settings (if applicable)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

- Email: support@gravitypaint.com

## Consent

By using Gravity Paint, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
